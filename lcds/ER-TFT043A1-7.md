# ER-TFT043A1-7 (4.3" 800x480 IPS Touch)

A reliable 4.3-inch IPS display with 800x480 resolution and integrated I²C capacitive touchscreen.

## Specifications
- Resolution: 800x480 pixels
- Size: 4.3 inches
- Panel Type: IPS (wide viewing angles)
- Touch: I²C capacitive (FT5406 controller)
- Power: 5V backlight, 3.3V logic
- Connector: 40-pin 0.5mm FPC (standard DPI compatible)
- Interface: RGB DPI (21-bit or 24-bit supported)
